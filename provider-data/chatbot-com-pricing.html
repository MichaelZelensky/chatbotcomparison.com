Frequently Asked Questions

How many chatbots can I create?

You can create as many chatbots as needed, but the number of active ones depends on your pricing plan.

Can I use ChatBot on several websites?

Yes, you can use your chatbots on multiple websites.

What happens when I exceed my chats package?

After you exceed your chats package, you'll be charged $0.03 per additional chat. We don't count empty chats or chats your chatbot hasn't responded to.

What payment forms do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

What happens after my trial ends?

You lose access to your ChatBot dashboard. However, your data and setup remain intact and safe. To log in to ChatBot again, you must provide your credit card details.

Can I respond to chats that my chatbot handles?

Yes. ChatBot can transfer a chat to a human agent in LiveChat or create a ticket in HelpDesk.

Where do you store data?

ChatBot stores its customers' information in a data center located near Dublin (Ireland).

How many chatbots can I activate?

The number of active chatbots depends on your plan. An active chatbot is one that is connected to one of the channel integrations.

What is a paid chat?

It's a chat that contains at least one successful interaction between the user and the chatbot. You'll never pay for spam chats.

How can I get an invoice?

After each successful payment, we'll email you an invoice at the provided email address.

Is there any contract required?

No, there are no contracts or hidden fees. You can cancel anytime.

Do I need to provide my credit card details to register?

We don't require your credit card details to start your free trial. We'll need them when your trial ends, and you decide to upgrade to a paid plan. You can unsubscribe anytime.

What languages does ChatBot support?

ChatBot lets you build bots in any language. The ChatBot dashboard is available only in English.

Do you provide tips on how to create a chatbot?

You can browse our Help Center and watch video tutorials to get started.
